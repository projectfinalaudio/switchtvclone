Mulamwah Finds Missing Kartelo

Posted on Friday, June 4, 2021


David Oyando, alias Mulamwah was a guy on a mission. On Thursday he went all out to look for Nick Chege, aka Kartelo. This was after Kartelo’s fans asked about his whereabouts. 

Kartelo has been missing in action for a while now. His last post on Instagram was on the 6th of February. For an artist, social media ranks high in priority and therefore Kartelo’s disappearance from the platforms got fans wondering whether everything with him was okay.  

Mulamwah’s quest was not in vain. The two are seen having a conversation with Kartelo babysitting his daughter in a video shared by Mulamwah. On Mulamwa’s enquiry as to why Kartelo has been off the public sphere, the latter responds by saying that he is okay and everything is fine. According to him, being away from social media doesn’t mean that there is a problem. 

"Mimi niko tu, unajua apart from kuwa msanii mimi ni baba ya msee…so hakuna ngori, kutokuwa social media sio ati kuna ngori. Sai unajua mimi ni mzazi na lazima nitake care of family Kwanza na sai mtoi ako na mwaka na week moja. so wasee waniwait tu narudi.” 



Before going off the grid, Kartelo used to work as a presenter at a local radio station. When Mulamwah asked him about his radio job, he became hesitant to respond, declaring that to be a story for another day.  

 “Radio nayo ni story ya siku ingine…lakini iko tu fine. Wasee wapende kupiga mboga yao safi… appreciate God kwa kile wako nayo, progress pole pole.” 

The pandemic has affected almost everyone in the country, and artists are at the top of the list. Could Kartelo be fine as he claims, or could he be undergoing a difficult time due to the pandemic or any other reason for that matter? What do you think?
