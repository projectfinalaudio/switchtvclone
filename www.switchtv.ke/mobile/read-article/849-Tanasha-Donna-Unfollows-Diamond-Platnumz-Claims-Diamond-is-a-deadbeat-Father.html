Tanasha Donna Unfollows Diamond Platnumz. Claims Diamond is a deadbeat Father

Posted on Thursday, June 3, 2021


Kenyan musician and the most followed influencer on Instagram, Tanasha Donna Oketch, has unfollowed Diamond Platnumz both on her personal Instagram account and that of their son Naseeb Junior. Her actions were prompted by claims that Diamond is a deadbeat father. 

Tanasha’s unhappiness with her baby daddy did not only end up in unfollowing him but also deleting every single picture of Diamond together with their son from her son’s social media accounts.  

Over the past few days, Tanasha gave netizens something to think about when she indirectly implied that Diamond has failed in his duties as a father. 

“Respect to all the mothers who do it all for their Kids.”  

Apparently, Tanasha unfollowing Diamond is not the first time. She once unfollowed him only to follow him again in November 2020, leaving her fans in a state of surprise.  



The two began dating in November 2018. A year later, they were blessed with their son Naseeb Junior. According to Diamond, Tanasha was the woman he had plans of getting married to until they broke up due to differences in their future preparations.  

 “I seriously wanted to marry Tanasha at 100 per cent or even one million per cent and that was why if someone spoke bad of her, I would simply post her picture as a clear message of how I love and respect her.”  

After months of physical distance, Tanasha took her son Naseeb Jnr to Tanzania to spend some quality time with his father early this year. It appeared as if the two were effectively co-parenting until now.
