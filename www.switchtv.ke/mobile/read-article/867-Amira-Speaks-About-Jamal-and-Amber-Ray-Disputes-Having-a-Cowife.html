Amira Speaks About Jamal and Amber Ray. Disputes Having a Cowife

Posted on Monday, June 21, 2021


Self-proclaimed Ambassador of Positivity, businesswoman and the first wife of Jamal Rohosafi, Amira aka Being Amira, has disputed the fact that Amber Ray is her cowife. 

Speaking on Switch TV Kenya’s chatspot, Amira stated that she like everybody else learnt about Jamal and Amber’s wedding on social media and hasn’t seen any marriage documents. 

“Swezi sema nina mke mwenza. Bado sitambui.” 

More than a week ago, Amira and Amber were seen exchanging accusations on social media. As Amira was trying to inform netizens about what was going on, Amber was disclosing too much personal information on Amira, information that were too intimate to be out in the public.

In the whole drama, Amber responded to her fans' curious questions accusing Amira of spending five years in Dubai working but still coming back home empty handed. 

“Mtu unaenda ku hustle majuu for 5 years na bado unarudi Kenya bila kakitu…surely ata kijiko? Alafu useme ndio sina akili…aje?” 



She also accused Amira of abandoning her babies for five years while enjoying herself in Dubai simply because their husband had no money. 

“Anyway, what do you expect from a woman who left her babies for 5 years, aende kurombosa Dubai..simply because our husband hakua na kakitu..and again kama yeye ni mzuri sana mbona hakupata bwana mzungu huko juu hao ndio sio polygamous….hapa Africa kushare ni lazima…” 

Amira responded to these claims stating that she went to Dubai specifically to work for the sake of her two children. According to her, Dubai is a four-hour flight from Kenya, and she would constantly come back to see her kids. 

Apparently, the information Amber shared on Amira was personal in a sense that she could have only shared it with her husband. How did Amber come across it? When Amira was asked if she has confronted her husband over the issue, she stated that they do talk. She went ahead to say that she did not feel betrayed but disrespected over the fact that her husband disclosed such information to Amber Ray.
