Vera Sidika Expecting First Child with Husband Brown Mauzo

Posted on Thursday, June 17, 2021


Kenyan socialite and businesswoman Vera Sidika is pregnant with her first child. Overwhelmed, Sidika expressed her joy on instagram where she posted a video of her baby bump for her fans to see. On the caption, she described how in love she was with her husband, Brown Mauzo. 

“6.06.2021 ...An adventure is about to begin...I love you so much my Husband @brownmauzo254 ????.” 

Vera stated that from the day she and her husband discovered that they would be parents, she doesn’t get to do any house chores since her man wouldn’t let her. He treats her like an egg, she mentioned.  

“Bae is the most amazing human ever. And even more now, ever since we found out we will soon be having out bundle of joy. Even my smooth preggo journey, he’s gone beyond to treat me like an egg. I don’t touch anything. He makes sure I eat fruits daily. Even though ain’t a fun. He feeds me by fire by force. Cooks for me, rubs my feet. Sometimes, I would wake up at 4am and mention I’m having slight back pain, BAE would get up 4am, massage my Back until I’m fine.” 

 



Netizens were attracted by Sidika’s announcement. Lots of congratulatory remarks followed as many told her how lovely a baby bump looked on her. 

Vera and Brown Mauza got married on 12th October 2020. Some of her fans told her that she had settled for less. In her response, money is the least of her concerns when it comes to her relationships. According to her, dating millionaires and billionaires in the past wasn’t as easy as people may anticipate. 

Vera is an ambitious woman and a successful businesswoman. She has had some on and off relationships which she described as not good. Now that she has found who she claims to be the one, would you say that Vera has found true love?
