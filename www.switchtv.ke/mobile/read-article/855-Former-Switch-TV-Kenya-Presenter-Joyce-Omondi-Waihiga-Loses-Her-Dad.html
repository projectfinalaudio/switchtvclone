Former Switch TV Kenya Presenter Joyce Omondi Waihiga Loses Her Dad

Posted on Wednesday, June 9, 2021


Former Switch TV Kenya presenter and Full Circle host Joyce Omondi Waihiga has lost her father. On her social media pages, Joyce paid the deepest tribute to her father as she shared the sad news. She eulogized her father, terming him as a man who had all it took and everything she needed in a father. 

"Among the greatest titles of my life is the privilege of being the daughter of this incredibly great man. He is everything I could ever pray for and desire in a father. The profound depth of his love, care, support and covering is the kind only few are privileged to receive. There is an enormous hole in my heart now that our heavenly father whom he taught me to love and serve has called him home. Only God can fill this void because the legacy of who my daddy was is almost larger than life. Yet, precious in the sight of the Lord is the death of His saints (PsaIm 116:15). So I know the sound of his endearing laugh is ringing throughout the streets of gold and one day I will feel the warmth of his loving embrace for eternity. March on great soldier. Sibuor wetu. I love you, daddy."  



Many sent their condolences messages including renowned media personalities Victoria Rubadiri and Yvonne Okwara.  

“Praying for you and your family, Joyce. May God uphold you, comfort you and cover you at this time ????????.” Wrote Victoria Rubadiri.  

“My deepest and most sincere condolences to you Joyce and your family..may your father rest in eternal peace may God give you peace..” Posted Kirigo Ngarua.  

“So very sorry for your loss. May you find comfort in the love and memories you shared. Praying for you and the entire family.” Posted Yvonne Okwara.
