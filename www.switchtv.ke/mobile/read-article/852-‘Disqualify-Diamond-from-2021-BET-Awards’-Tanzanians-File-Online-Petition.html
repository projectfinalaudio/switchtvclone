‘Disqualify Diamond from 2021 BET Awards’. Tanzanians File Online Petition

Posted on Monday, June 7, 2021


Renowned musician Diamond Platnumz is a worried man! An online partition has been launched by a section of Tanzanians appealing for Diamond Platnumz to be disqualified from the forthcoming 2021 BET Awards. 

Marian Sarungi Tsehai is the founder of this movement that seeks to ensure Diamond’s chances to become Best International Act at the BET Awards become most unlikely. According to the movement, Diamond Platnumz was in active support of the late President John Pombe Magufuli’s regime, which the petitioners termed oppressive. 

In 2017-2018 under president Magufuli’s rule, part of Tanzanian artists were arrested, abducted, and others imprisoned over claims that they participated in obscene video clips. Diamond was among the arrested artists after he shared a video of himself kissing a girl. However, what the petitioners are angry about is the fact that Diamond maintained silence instead of condemning this act. 



“Acha Nikae Kimya” is a track that Diamond Platnumz released in 2017 when the public was onto him to break the silence and condemn the suppressions. Up until now, Diamond has not spoken a word concerning the matter. 

Tanzanian politicians who are against LGBTQ have been categorized as oppressors of human rights. Diamond has been accused of befriending such leaders, and as such, the petitioners find more convincing reasons not to support him in the coming competition. 

The movement seeks to achieve 25k signatures to give the petition an upper hand. 16,400 signatures have been obtained as Tanzanians continue signing.  

BET has nominated Diamond under the Best International Act division and the only artist from East Africa. He is bound to face other artists Emicida from Brazil, both Nigeria’s Wizkid and Burna Boy and UK's Bugsey and Young T. The Awards are set to take place on June 28.
