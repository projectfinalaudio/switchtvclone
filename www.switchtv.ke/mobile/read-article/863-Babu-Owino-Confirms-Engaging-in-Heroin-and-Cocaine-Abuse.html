Babu Owino Confirms Engaging in Heroin and Cocaine Abuse

Posted on Thursday, June 17, 2021


Former SONU chairman and the current Member of Parliament for Embakasi East constituency Paul Ongili Owino aka Babu Owino has confirmed previous abuse of drugs including cocaine and heroin.  

Speaking in an interview, Babu said that he has equally consumed a wide variety of alcoholic substances. He went ahead to say that he stopped taking alcohol which, according to him, gave him too much energy that he never used for the right reasons. 

“It made me have a lot of energy and I used that energy in the wrong way." 

It appears like Babu’s interest in alcohol has not entirely faded. According to him, traditional liquor such as Muratina, chang'aa, and busaa should be legalized.  

"But when it comes to these traditional brews, I think they should be legalized because when we have weddings you see these muratina, chang'aa, busaa and the likes."  

Over the past, Babu’s insulting remarks about individuals in the political opposition left netizens wondering if he was sobber or under the influence. During a National Super Alliance (NASA) campaign, Babu insulted the president and likened him with previous African presidents who used dictatorship policies.  

"If Muammar Gaddafi (Libya) was removed by citizens if Laurent Gbagbo (Ivory Coast) were removed by citizens if Yahya Jammeh (Gambia) was removed by citizens, who are you? Wewe ni mtoto wa mbwa (you are the son of a dog)." 



A video footage of him pulling out a gun and shooting a DJ at a local club surfaced online. In the video, Babu is seen shooting Felix Orinda aka DJ Evolve and does not even flinch. DJ Evolve was recently rumored dead. He shared a video of him on social media confirming that he is alive and well. 

Babu revealed having engaged in drug abuse in his past life but was not specific on the particular period. He also explained how alcohol impaired his judgment and found no benefit in its use.  

“Personally, I've consumed everything but I didn't see the benefit of it because it almost led to my destruction. I have done a lot of drugs, I've done cocaine, heroin and many brands of alcohol but that was in my last life."
