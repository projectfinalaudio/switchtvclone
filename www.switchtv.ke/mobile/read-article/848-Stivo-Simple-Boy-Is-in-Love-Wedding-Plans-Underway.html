Stivo Simple Boy Is in Love. Wedding Plans Underway

Posted on Tuesday, June 1, 2021


Renowned Kenyan musician Stephene Otieno, alias Stivo Simple Boy is officially off the market. Three years ago, Stivo, best known for his educative music rose to fame after his song Vijana Tuache Mihadarati went viral. 

To Stivo, true love has always been difficult to find. Ever since his breakthrough, many ladies have tried to get his attention in vain. He found no reason to make haste into a relationship and preferred to take his time to find true love. His patience has finally paid off. 

Speaking in an interview, Stivo share a beautiful story of how he found love. For ladies who have been eyeing him, sorry to disappoint you but Stivo is taken. Currently, he is dating a lady from Kibra whom he met on the road. 

"I met her on the road and approached her. I told her 'nimekuzimia na tena nimekudata.”  

Fridah, as Stivo referred to her, is a lady that he took his time to study and get to know better. According to him, his decision to wed her is based on careful analysis.  

"Siwezi penda mtu mara moja mpaka nijue tabia zake”.  



Stivo’s wedding plans are underway. He met Fridah’s brother to show his interest in marrying her and permission was granted with the condition that he only sticks to Fridah and not eye other women. 

"I met her brother because her parents' live upcountry and I told him I was interested in his sister. He told me its okay as long as nisikuwe umbwa mwitu, leo niko na huyu kesho niko na ule.”  

Stivo stated that as soon as things get back to normal, the wedding will take place. 

"Harusi tutapanga wacha tuone vile hii Coronvirus ikipungua”.  

Stivo Simple Boy is not the only renowned Kenyan musician planning to tie the knot. Recently, renowned gospel artist Gurdian Angel proposed to his 51-year-old girl friend Esther Musila. The two are yet to walk down the aisle.
