Xtian Dela on the spot for endorsing pyramid scheme like crypto

Posted on Saturday, June 19, 2021


Overtime, pyramid schemes have become a new normal in the country.  A pyramid scheme is a fraudulent system of making money based on recruiting an ever-increasing number of  "investors."  The initial promoters recruit  investors, who in turn recruit more investors, and so on. The scheme is called a "pyramid" because at each level, the number of investors increases. The small group of initial promoters at the top require a large base of later investors to support the scheme by providing profits to the earlier investors.Latest in the market we have Paper wave. This is a ‘ponzi’ that presents itself as a Kenyan crypto trading platform but in reality it operates on trading in built coins and referrals. Only those with huge audiences and those who join early are able to make money. Kenyan blogger Author Mandela aka Xtian Dela is at the forefront ,championing for this scheme and is presenting it as Kenya's first crypto.

 

Netizens came out in plenty to share their opinions on Edgar Obare’s instagram page. Some revealed how they made money and some explained how they lost their money through the scheme. Those who claim to be crypto experts bashed the paper wave platform claiming that is not how crypto works. The coin that they claim to trade is known as stellar but the payment on paper wave goes to STKELLA ENTERPRISES.



Xtian Dela has since defended the coin and even claimed that it is doing so good that is why it is experiencing technical hitches and that a team of experts have been recruited to solve the issue.

 

“Kenyans have broken the paper wave website …everyone is buying…The website just went down for some users…just spoke to Brian and the team.They just recruited a whole team of customer care agents and are currently moving servers for a more stable experience.”

 

The blogger is not new to such schemes with some netizens claiming that he was involved in a similar ‘ponzi’ scheme that was earlier in the year known as earn smart. As per now the paper wave site is down citing a shift of servers which is how they disappear. We can only wait and see how this will turn out. So, is paper wave Kenya's first crypto or another pyramid scheme?
