Real Talk Round Table is a spinoff of the show Real Talk with Tamima. Just like the predecessor, the show intends to continue having real conversations with real people who have lived and experienced issues affecting society. The show takes it further through calls to action and a challenge to society to be part of the change. The Host Tamima Ibrahim fondly refers to the round table as the table of transformation.
