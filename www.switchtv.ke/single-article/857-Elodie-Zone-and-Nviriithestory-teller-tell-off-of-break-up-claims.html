Elodie Zone and Nviriithestory teller tell off of break up claims

Share Tweet

Posted on Friday, June 11, 2021

A few says a go you-tuber Elodie zone set the internet ablaze with claims that her relationship with Nvirii had come to an end through a post on her Instagram stories .The You-tuber further implied that the relationship was toxic.
 
 

“ Physical, mental, verbal and emotional abuse towards women is unacceptable! I want to make it clear that I am NOT dating Nviiri . We just met at the Coast, but the reason that I left him is the same reason that has come up as we are “respectful friends”. I’ve worked too damn hard as a woman to love myself. To be confident. To  stack my money. To support those around me.” 

In a surprise turn of events Elodie zone has refuted claim that the relationship is over.  In a post made on Instagram, she blamed it on alcohol and tension.

“Nvirii and i came to cast with our friends to unwind,relax and make peace with each other when i made my post it was fueled by tension and an afternoon of a few too many drinks.”

She further stated that she did not expect that the news would be exaggerated as it was. She blames the turn of events on the overzealousness of bloggers and blogging sites.

“ Neither of us thought it would be blown out of proportion the way it has been. Blogging sites and bloggers have also began to spread misinformation and this is affecting our career and family lives."
Nvirii also defended the relationship in a phone interview with a local radio station, terming the situation as a small disagreement and that everything was okay back home.

“Kwani tumeachana , hujaona kwa Instagram bado tunapostiana ...vita ndogo zisiwashtue ... utafanya niweke picha yake na yangu sahii hapa niwakasirishe.”

Nvirii’s record label Sol Generation Records released a statement surrounding the nature of his relationship and assured fans that they do not condone any form of mental ,physical and emotional abuse.

“ As sol generation we do not and will not tolerate any form of mental physical and emotional abuse. The recent events and allegations surrounding Nvirii The Story teller and Elodie are of great concern to us. Over the last 48 hours we have been working frantically to understand and investigate these events. The sensitivity of this matter should not be understated ,and us such we have been treating this matter with the upmost privacy and humanity.”
