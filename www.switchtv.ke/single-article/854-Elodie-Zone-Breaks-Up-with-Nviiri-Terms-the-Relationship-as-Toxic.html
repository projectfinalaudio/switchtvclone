Elodie Zone Breaks Up with Nviiri. Terms the Relationship as Toxic

Share Tweet

Posted on Wednesday, June 9, 2021

Netizens have been left guessing after YouTuber Elodie Zone stormed social media with some shocking information on the state of her relationship with Nviiri the Storyteller. She made it clear that they had broken up, terming the relationship as abusive. 

Elodie had shared posts of them at the airport and fans thought the two were still in a relationship. Their relationship status became clearer on Sunday night when she confirmed during a Question-and-Answer session that she was no longer dating Nviiri over claims that he physically abused her.  

“Physical, mental, verbal and emotional abuse towards women is unacceptable! I want to make it clear that I am NOT dating Nviiri. We just met at the Coast, but the reason that I left him is the same reason that has come up as we are “respectful friends”. I’ve worked too damn hard as a woman to love myself. To be confident. Tos atck my money. To support those around me.” 

She went ahead to say that she owes no man anything and continued engaging in the QnA session with her fans. 

Last year in October, Elodie mentioned an incident where she became a victim of sexual harassment. According to her, the person she thought was her friend and could save her from that defenseless position abandoned her. 

“Ladies, have friends and be with a man that’ll protect you. You never want to hit the ground unsure of who will and won’t be there to catch you. My experience last night was an eye opener. The closest person to me left me in a position where I could have died. I would never wish that on anyone!” 

It wasn’t clear then whether the ‘friend’ Elodie made reference to was Nviiri. However, she revealed that Nviiri was indeed the man. He allegedly left her at the roadside at night past curfew hours not caring how she would get home. She also revealed that he once physically attacked her at a party they had attended together.

Elodie also revealed that she hasn’t had a good experience in her previous two relationships. She stated that abuse was part of her past relationships but was asked to be silent about it. Elodie once dated Sean Andrews, Kibaki’s grandson. The two separated in 2016 over toxicity claims.  

However, after Elodie denying dating Nviiri, he confirmed during an interview that they were still a thing. 

"We have been friends for eight years. We kept our relationship a secret for months without telling people we were dating. We needed to understand ourselves, are we strong enough for the public because they can be ruthless."Our decision was to keep it low until we were ready to announce it. It affects our brands in a way...will I still continue being Nviiri the ladies' man."
